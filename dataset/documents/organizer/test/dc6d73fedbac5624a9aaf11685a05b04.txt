SECTION 1. Amendments to the First Lien ISDA Master Agreement. Effective on and after the Effective Date (as defined below) the First Lien ISDA Master Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
1.1 If, and only if, Party A and Party B have not entered into a definitive written agreement regarding RINs and environmental credits on or before January 15, 2022, Part 16(a) of the First Lien ISDA Master Agreement shall be automatically amended without further action effective on and after January 16, 2022, as follows:
(a) By inserting the following new clause (xxii) at the end of Part 16(a) of the First Lien ISDA Master:
"(xxii) Liens which secure a Permitted RINs Transaction; provided, that such Liens are only granted with respect to RINs financed between or sold and repurchased under such Permitted RINs Transaction and the identifiable proceeds thereof.
(b) By inserting the following new clause (viii) immediately after clause (vii) of Part 16(c) of the First Lien ISDA Master:
"(viii) obligations arising from Permitted RINs Transactions to the extent constituting Indebtedness."
(c) By inserting the following new clause (x) at the end of Part 16(h) of the First Lien ISDA Master:
"(x) sales of RINs pursuant to Permitted RINs Transactions to the extent constituting Dispositions."
NY 78795444v17
(d) By inserting the following new defined term in Part 20 of the First Lien ISDA Master in the appropriate alphabetical order:
""Permitted RINs Transaction" means a repurchase agreement or financing agreement with respect to RINs entered into by Party B and a third party with respect to which each of the following is true:
i. immediately prior to and after giving effect to such transaction, no Event of Default, Potential Event of Default or Termination Event has occurred and is continuing under the First Lien ISDA Master Agreement or any other Transaction Document;
ii. the obligations with respect to such transaction are only secured by a Lien on the RINs financed or purchased pursuant to such transaction and any identifiable proceeds with respect thereto; and
iii. as of any date, such repurchase agreement or financing agreement may not have an aggregate sale and repurchase price or maximum principal amount (as applicable) in an amount greater than the value of the RINs (inclusive of the value of any hedge transaction entered into to hedge price risk with respect to such RINs under such repurchase or financing agreement) at any time for longer than three (3) Business Days."
1.2 Effective on and after the Effective Date, the following defined term "Term Expiry Date" in Part 20 of the First Lien ISDA Master Agreement is hereby deleted and replaced in its entirety with the following new definition:
""Term Expiry Date" means December 21, 2022."
1.3 Effective on and after the Effective Date, Attachment 6 (Applicable Curves) to the First Lien ISDA Master Agreement is deleted in its entirety and replaced with Exhibit A hereto.
