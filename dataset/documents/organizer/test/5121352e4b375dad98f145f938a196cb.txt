If, on any day on which the weighted average Mortgage Rate applicable to the Mortgage Loans forming part of the Assets of the Series Trust which are charged interest at a variable rate is equal to or greater than the then Threshold Rate, Party A notifies Party B and each Rating Agency of its intention to terminate the Basis Swap.
